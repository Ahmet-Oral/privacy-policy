UtiliTrack – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 May 2026

Overview

UtiliTrack is a personal utility bill tracker. All data you enter is stored locally on your device only. No data is ever transmitted to any server, third party, or developer.

Data We Collect

None. UtiliTrack does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no internet connection and no analytics.

Data Stored on Your Device

Meter readings and bill costs you enter are saved in a local SQLite database on your device. This data never leaves your device.

Permissions Used

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — Used only to send you local reminders to submit a new meter reading. No notification data is sent to any server.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — Used to reschedule local notifications after the device restarts. No data is transmitted.

Third-Party Services

None. UtiliTrack does not integrate any analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or cloud services.

Children's Privacy

UtiliTrack is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from anyone.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
